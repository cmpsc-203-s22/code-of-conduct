#### Acceptable Conduct

- Students should arrive to class sessions, practical sessions and lab sessions on time. These times are:
Class Session: Monday and Wednesday from 10:40 am - 11:40 am
Practical Session: Friday from 10:40 am - 11:40 am
Laboratory Session: Tuesday from 2:50 pm - 4:40 pm
- Students should give about one-week prior notice if they are late to class, have conflicts with other classes, or are going to be absent before the class begins.
- Students should come to class prepared by completing labs, practicals, and reading discord to know any information needed for lessons.
- Students who are late should check in with the professor and classmates after class in order to stay caught up with work.
